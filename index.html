How to Deep Link in Cordova, PhoneGap, and Ionic

Why add deep linking to your app

URLs can navigate to your website, but not to your app. Deep Links allow users to install, open, and pass data into your app. Deep linking will increase app discovery and increasing overall app engagement.

Test deep linking in iOS

  • Create a deep link from the Branch Dashboard
  • Delete your app from the device
  • Compile your app (cordova run ios)
  • Paste deep link in Apple Notes
  • Long press on the deep link (not 3D Touch)
  • Click Open in "APP_NAME" to open your app (example)

Test deep linking in Android

  • Create a deep link from the Branch Dashboard
  • Delete your app from the device
  • Compile your app (cordova run android)
  • Paste deep link in Google Hangouts
  • Click on the deep link to open your app
